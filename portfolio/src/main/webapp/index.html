About Me

My name is Jacob Chidawaya, a Zimbawean that was born and raised in South Africa. I am a rising sophmore at the University of Pennsylvania majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Philsophy and Consumer Psychology. This summer I am a Google Step intern looking to gain insight into a potential career path whilst developing my professional etiquette.

I went to a choir school for three years where I was able to cultivate my creative interests. After that I finished my high school career at an all boys boarding school where sports were highly popular. These two experiences inspired the work I will be sharing in this portfolio. Flowerboy is my account of what Tyler The Creator's album "Flowerboy" means to me and the impression it has had. Ballers is an image grid of my soccer interests with the teams/players that have been most influential for me.

Flowerboy

Amongst Tyler's albums my easy going demeanour is best matched by Flowerboy. Before the first note is sound the album cover depicts a vivid scenery which is oddly soothing. The bright background contrasts the figure standing with folded arms whilst facing away from the viewer. The massive bees show that this is not realistic and verify the attractiveness of the Flowerboy. I imagine that Tyler gave us a glimpse into his extraordinary imagination without allowing us to loose sense of his calm character. The music continues to build the narrative he portrayed on the cover by by mixing a variety of songs which blend to share Tyler's current views. He is somehow composed on this album whilst discussing his depression, lonliness and love stories. The legitimacy of his sentiment is conveyed in his unconventional chord progressions and ertaic developments. By not allowing us to know what comes next Tyler makes us experience his unstelled feelings. The style is uniquely his own with inspiration drawn from the many artists he features. This album remains amongst my favourite listens and I could play See You Again on repeat for hours. I like to think that I could be described as a Flowerboy, both expressive and calm

Ballers

The following is a collection of players amd teams who have inspired my love for the game. Soccer is the most popular sport in the world. This popularity means there are many boys like me who grow up loving the game and wishing to one day ascend to the heights where their skills can be admired alongside the greatest players of their generation. Football stars receive contracts from major brands and can be seen in many adverts that are not related to soccer. Their prowess on the field has allowed them to captivate billions of fans across the world. Evidently, the World Cup is the most-watched overall live event on television worldwide. Soccer brings nations together. The sport is a common language understood universally. The joys and heartache felt are a testiment of people's love for the beautiful game.
